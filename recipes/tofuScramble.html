Tofu Scramble

Easy to make breakfast staple that is a great substitution for scrambled eggs

Ingredients

  • Tofu
  • Frozen spinach
  • Canned tomato chunks
  • Olive oil

Directions

  1. Thaw the frozen block of spinach
  2. Lightly coat the pan with olive oil
  3. Cut the tofu up into small chunks
  4. Add the chunked tofu, spinach and tomato chunks to pan
  5. Cook till most moisture from ingredients have evaporated
  6. Serve with a side of toast or in a burrito
