Soy Milk

Delicious milk althernative made from soybeans

Ingredients

  • Soybeans
  • Water
  • Sugar (optional)

Directions

  1. Soak soybeans overnight
  2. Drain and add to blender with water
  3. Blend till almost smooth
  4. Strain in a pot with a nutmilk bag
  5. Boil and stir for 20 minutes
  6. Add sugar to taste
  7. Let cool and enjoy
