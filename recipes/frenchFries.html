French Fries

An American classic side dish

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Vegetable oil

Directions

  1. Wash Potatoes
  2. Slice into thin long rectangles
  3. Dry off the extra moisture
  4. Heat oil up in a pan or deep fryer
  5. Add potatoes to oil
  6. Cook until fries are a golden brown
  7. Remove fries from oil and add to plate lined with paper towel
  8. Salt to taste
